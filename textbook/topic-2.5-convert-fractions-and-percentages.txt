LEARNING OBJECTIVES
-------------------
- Convert fractions to percentages
- Convert percentages to fractions
- Find percentages of objects and quantities

KEY CONCEPTS
------------

1. WHAT IS PERCENTAGE?

Percentage means "out of 100". The symbol % means percent.
35 out of 100 is 35 hundredths, which is 35%.

100 doughnuts were sold. 35 pieces were chocolate doughnuts.
So 35% of the doughnuts were chocolate.

2. CONVERTING FRACTIONS TO PERCENTAGES

Method 1: Convert the fraction to an equivalent fraction with denominator 100.
  Example: 7/10 = 70/100 = 70%

Method 2: Multiply the fraction by 100%.
  Example: 7/10 × 100% = 70%

3. CONVERTING PERCENTAGES TO FRACTIONS

Write the percentage as a fraction out of 100, then simplify.

Example: 40% = 40/100 = 2/5 (simplified by dividing numerator and denominator by 20)

WORKED EXAMPLES
---------------

Example 1: State the percentage of the chocolate doughnuts.

Answer:
35 out of 100 doughnuts are chocolate.
35 hundredths = 35%
The percentage of the chocolate doughnuts is 35%.

Example 2: Write 7/10 in percentage.

Answer:
Method 1: 
7/10 = (7 × 10)/(10 × 10) = 70/100 = 70%

Method 2:
7/10 × 100% = 70%

So 7/10 = 70%.

Example 3: Convert 4/5 to percentage.

Answer:
Method 1: 
4/5 = (4 × 20)/(5 × 20) = 80/100 = 80%

Method 2:
4/5 × 100% = 80%

So 4/5 = 80%.

Example 4: Write 40% as a fraction in simplest form.

Answer:
40% = 40/100
Simplify: Divide numerator and denominator by 20.
40 ÷ 20 = 2
100 ÷ 20 = 5
So 40% = 2/5.

Example 5: Write 63% as a fraction of hundredths.

Answer:
63% = 63/100

Example 6: Write 90% as a fraction in simplest form.

Answer:
90% = 90/100
Simplify: Divide by 10.
90 ÷ 10 = 9
100 ÷ 10 = 10
So 90% = 9/10.

Example 7: Finding percentage of objects.

3 out of 10 hats are red.
The fraction of red hats is 3/10.
Percentage of red hats = 3/10 × 100% = 30%

8 vans out of 40 vehicles:
Percentage = 8/40 × 100% = 20%

Aimi read 12 out of 50 books.
Percentage = 12/50 × 100% = 24%

KEY VOCABULARY
--------------
- Percentage: A number out of 100, shown with the % symbol
- Percent: Means "out of 100" or "per hundred"
- Fraction: A part of a whole, shown as numerator/denominator
- Convert: To change from one form to another (fraction to percentage or vice versa)
- Equivalent fraction: A fraction that has the same value but different numerator and denominator
- Simplify: To reduce a fraction to its smallest possible numerator and denominator
- Hundredths: Parts out of 100
