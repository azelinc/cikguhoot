LEARNING OBJECTIVES
-------------------
- Solve word problems involving addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
- Apply problem-solving steps: Understand, Plan, Solve, Check
- Solve problems involving unknowns

KEY CONCEPTS
------------

1. STEPS FOR SOLVING PROBLEMS

Step 1: Understand the problem
- Read carefully and underline important information.

Step 2: Plan the strategy
- Decide what operation(s) to use.
- Draw a diagram if it helps.

Step 3: Solve
- Write the number sentence.
- Calculate the answer.

Step 4: Check
- Check if the answer is reasonable.
- Use estimation to verify.
- Do the inverse operation to check.

2. ADDITION

Add according to place value. Start from ones.
Group numbers that make 10 to add faster.

3. SUBTRACTION

Subtract according to place value. Start from ones.
Borrow from the next place value when needed.

4. FINDING UNKNOWNS

Use the inverse operation to find unknown values.
If 18 + n = 30, then n = 30 - 18 = 12.

Example 1: Addition (Page 26)
The table shows items sold in a year:
- Hoverboard: 7,237
- Skateboard: 653
- Scooter: 9,084
- Mini car: 18,045

What is the total number of skateboards and hoverboards sold?

Answer:
18,045 + 7,237 = ?
    1  8, 0 4 5
+      7, 2 3 7
   2 5, 2 8 2

The total number of skateboards and hoverboards sold is 25,282.

Example 2: Addition of three numbers (Page 27)
Find the total number of mini cars, scooters, and skateboards.

Answer:
653 + 9,084 + 18,045 = ?
        1 8, 0 4 5
          9, 0 8 4
+            6 5 3
       2 7, 7 8 2

The total is 27,782.

Example 3: Subtraction (Page 31)
Number of UPSR candidates:
- SK: 26,467
- Private School: 1,117
- SJKC: 3,429
- SJKT: 1,295

a) How much less is the number of candidates in private school compared to SK?
   26,467 - 1,117 = 25,350

b) Calculate the difference between SK and SJKT.
   26,467 - 1,295 = 25,172

Example 4: Area Problem (Page 29)
The total area of Sabah, Melaka, and Perlis is 76,116 km².
Sabah: 73,631 km², Melaka: 1,664 km²
Calculate the area of Perlis.

Answer:
73,631 + 1,664 + Perlis = 76,116
Perlis = 76,116 - 73,631 - 1,664
Perlis = 821 km²

Example 5: Unknown in Addition (Page 30)
18 + n = 30
What is the value of n?

Answer:
n = 30 - 18
n = 12

Check: 18 + 12 = 30 ✓

Example 6: Problem Solving with Multiplication (Page 61)
13 schools sent 24 participants each. Find the total number of participants.

Answer:
13 × 24 = 312
The total number of participants is 312.

Example 7: Problem Solving with Division (Page 63)
A factory produces 14,186 powerbanks in a day. 32 powerbanks are put in each box.
How many boxes are needed? What is the remainder?

Answer:
14,186 ÷ 32 = 443 remainder 10
443 boxes are needed with 10 powerbanks left over.

Check: 443 × 32 = 14,176, then 14,176 + 10 = 14,186 ✓

Example 8: Mixed Operations (Page 64)
A poultry farm worker puts 84 cages of chickens into a lorry. Each cage has 
8 chickens. The cages are distributed equally to 7 markets. 
How many chickens are sent to each market?

Answer:
Total chickens = 84 × 8 = 672
Chickens per market = 672 ÷ 7 = 96
96 chickens are sent to each market.

Number sentence: 84 × 8 ÷ 7 = 96

Example 9: Find Unknown (Page 67)
Amirah has 32 pieces of biscuits. Jasmin has p pieces of biscuits.
Their total number of biscuits is 50 pieces. What is the value of p?

Answer:
32 + p = 50
p = 50 - 32
p = 18

KEY VOCABULARY
--------------
- Understand: Read and identify the important information in a problem
- Plan: Decide which operations to use to solve the problem
- Solve: Write the number sentence and calculate the answer
- Check: Verify if the answer is correct using the inverse operation
- Number sentence: A mathematical statement using numbers and operations
- Unknown: A missing value represented by a letter (like p, n, y)
- Inverse operation: Opposite operations (addition and subtraction, multiplication and division)
