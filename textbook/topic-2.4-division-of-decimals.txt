LEARNING OBJECTIVES
-------------------
- Divide decimals by whole numbers
- Divide decimals by 10, 100, and 1,000
- Place the decimal point correctly in the answer

KEY CONCEPTS
------------

1. DIVIDING DECIMALS BY WHOLE NUMBERS

Division of decimals is the same as division of whole numbers.
- Align the decimal point in the answer with the decimal point in the number being divided.
- Complete the division until there is no remainder (add zeros if needed).

2. DIVIDING DECIMALS BY 10, 100, AND 1,000

To divide by 10: Shift the decimal point 1 place to the LEFT.
To divide by 100: Shift the decimal point 2 places to the LEFT.
To divide by 1,000: Shift the decimal point 3 places to the LEFT.

This is because the value of the quotient decreases.

KEY VOCABULARY
--------------
- Divide: To split into equal parts or groups
- Quotient: The result of division
- Remainder: The amount left over (but with decimals, we continue until no remainder)
- Align: To line up decimal points vertically
- Shift: To move the decimal point to the left (for division by 10, 100, 1,000)
